RFOUNDATION WHO WE ARE CAREERS
Career
Reliance Foundation, the philanthropic arm of Reliance Industries Limited, aims to play a catalytic role in addressing the nation’s development challenges through innovative and sustainable solutions. Led by Founder and Chairperson Smt. Nita Ambani, Reliance Foundation is relentlessly working towards facilitating transformative changes to ensure overall wellbeing and higher quality of life for all. Reliance Foundation is focused on addressing the nation’s development challenges in Rural Transformation, Education, Health, Sports for Development, Women Empowerment, Disaster Management, Urban Renewal and Arts, Culture and Heritage, and has touched the lives of more than 6.4 crore (64 million) people across India, in more than 53,000 villages and several urban locations.
Reliance Foundation is an equal opportunity employer and we believe in the power of every individual’s potential. We seek highly motivated, qualified individuals who aspire to be changemakers and are passionate about impacting lives for a fulfilling career with us. Come join a team of highly accomplished professionals with a range of expertise across social development concerns.
If you want to be a part of the Reliance Foundation family, please mail us your CV at careers.rf@reliancefoundation.org.
We look forward to hearing from you!
